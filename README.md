## Overview

Dropshare Professional Suite is a complete file sharing and screenshot platform that serves professionals with the hosting flexibility, annotation tools, and sharing workflow needed for fast, self-hosted content sharing. It transforms the act of sharing files and screenshots from a multi-step process into an instant operation that respects your data ownership. The combination of capture, annotation, upload, and link generation in a single tool eliminates the fragmented workflow of using separate apps for each step.

Annotation tools mark up screenshots with arrows, shapes, text, highlights, and blur effects before upload, making it possible to communicate clearly through annotated screenshots without opening a separate image editor. Privacy blur ensures sensitive information visible in screenshots is obscured before sharing. The annotation interface is designed for speed, with quick-access tools that add context to a screenshot in seconds rather than minutes.

Custom domain support serves shared files from your own domain name rather than a generic cloud URL, presenting shared content under your professional brand rather than a third-party hosting provider's address. SSL certificates are configured automatically for custom domains, ensuring that shared links are secure and trustworthy. Landing pages for shared files can be customized with your branding, creating a polished sharing experience that reflects your professional identity.

Upload history and management tracks every shared file with timestamps, links, and preview thumbnails, making it easy to find and re-share previously uploaded content or clean up files that are no longer needed. Expiring links can be configured to automatically disable access after a set period, adding a layer of control to sensitive shared content. Batch management tools allow bulk deletion or link regeneration across your entire sharing history.

Dropshare Professional Suite is the file sharing tool for professionals who share content frequently enough that the speed, branding, and hosting control of a dedicated sharing tool is worth the investment over generic cloud sharing. Its self-hosted approach means your files live on your infrastructure, under your domain, with your privacy policies — not someone else's.

## Performance & Integration

Dropshare is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Dropshare processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
